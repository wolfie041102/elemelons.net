Alive:

  • Hades (HadesTheImmortal)

    Age: Immortal
    Gender: Male
    Divine: Yes

    Description:

    As one of the main 3 gods, Hades is one of the most powerful divine beings. He is the god of the dead, and king of the underworld. His purpose in this universe is unclear, but he has a massive kingdom he rules over together with his wife, Persephone. Hades is not reckless, and doesn’t kill without reason. When mortals ask him for a favor, he will ask for something back, or a way for the mortals to prove they are worthy of his favor. He does not like unwanted visitors in the underworld, and won’t treat them kindly.

    First appearance:

    Hades is first seen under the name ‘Proteus’ as one of Phobos’s guards, together with Alyx. As described before, they plan on taking him down when he is at his weakest. After that, they will retreat to the underworld for a while to gain power and come up with his next move.

  • Chymes (Wolfie041102)

    Age: Unknown
    Gender: Male
    Divine: No

    Description:

    Chymes is an alchemist better than anyone has ever seen. His alchemy skills are unmatched and his reputation mysterious. He seems to like experimenting on others, especially Skye.

    Backstory:

    In his younger years, Chymes worked for a king in a successful kingdom. He would cure any sickness, and supply the entire army with things that made them unstoppable. But this wasn’t enough for Chymes. He wanted more power, more succes. So he started experimenting on humans. When the King found out, he exiled Chymes. Mysteriously, the entire kingdom died that year from a mysterious disease. Ever since then, Chymes has worked for his own.

    First appearance:

  • Persephone (The_Persephone)

    Age: Immortal
    Gender: Female
    Divine: Yes

    Description:

    Persephone is the Goddess of spring and vegetation, Hades’ wife, and queen of the underworld. Formerly known as Kore, but no one calls her that, for good reason. For a long time many people worshipped her, but now they barely remember. However, she’s planning on making them remember. She rules besides Hades, and has about as much to say about the land as Hades himself, but prefers to leave any cases regarding mortals up to him. She is a kind and gentle person but when insulted, she will make sure the person gets punished in cruel ways. When her power is challenged she doesn’t take this lightly or kindly. She is not easy to reason with, unlike hades.

    First appearance:

    She will first be seen living on her own, not socializing a lot, but when needed or wanted. When Hades and Helen execute their plan, she’s eager to join them in the underworld, to restore her full power and status, and rule as queen.

Motives

Hero Motivation: Echo Robulet-Dagon

Echo’s reason for being the hero is out of revenge. She was very close to Phobos, and it hurt her a lot when he got killed. She found out that Huxley was the cause of Phobos’ death, and now she wants to end Huxley’s life as revenge.



Villain motivation: Huxley

Huxley got abused and betrayed over and over again by his parents and family. But after a while, he found a friend, Phobos. Huxley and Phobos were very close, and Phobos promised to protect Huxley from Hades. When Huxley eventually got attacked by Hades, Phobos was nowhere to be seen. This was the last drop for Huxley, and he went insane. Out of hopelessness Huxley sold his soul to Hades, so that Hades would kill Phobos. After he did, Huxley kept going in a downwards spiral, and wouldn’t stop until he dies.

Lives:

Upon joining the server, all players will receive 3 canon lives. These can only be taken away in roleplay, so mob kills or accidental deaths DONT count. Killing someone in RP has to be done with a valid motivation, otherwise the kill doesn’t count. You are NOT allowed to kill someone without warning, you have to make it dramatic, and announce to the victim that you are going to attempt to take one of their lives.

Special Ones:

Special ones are people with game altering abilities



Immortal: Hades

There is an immortal that is only killable with special weapons that they get to choose themselves. The immortal has 1 specific weapon, so not all special weapons will work on all immortals. The immortals get to choose what to do with their own weapon, wether to hide it somewhere and make a story around it, or keep it in their own possession, or lost.



Mind control: Persephone

When the mind controler tells someone to do something (in a way they get to decide), the victim HAS to listen, unless specified differently.



Shape shifting: Echo Robulet-Dagon

The shape shifter can turn into any animal (or person!) at any time, but it will exhaust them. She does the shape shifting by telling people around her that she is in a different form, and they have to act accordingly



Necromancer: Char

The necromancer can communicate and interact with people that are canonically dead. (And maybe resurrect?)



Alchemic manifestation: Skye

The Alchemic manifestor is experimented on by the Alchemist. This has left them with some permanent side effects, these are: making others hallucinate, feel the precense of objects, or making people feel pain or other sensations.
