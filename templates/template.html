The Elemelons server:

Welcome to the Elemelons server!

The Elemelons server is a survival Roleplay server for Minecraft, created by Wolfie041102 and HadesTheImmortal. On this server you will find a community that is absolutely addicted to Minecraft, and quite commited to the roleplay. The roleplay you will see on this server is NOT scripted, except for some general outlines on the main story. Feel free to join, build a base, meet the characters, and get involved in the roleplay! And most importanly, Have Fun!
