  • How do I join?

    • Since the server uses a whitelist, you’re allowed to join if one of the members invites you, and one of the owners approves.

    If you're whitelisted then follow these steps:
    1. In Minecraft, go to multiplayer
    2. Click “add server” at the bottom of your screen
    3. Type "elemelons.net" in the Server Address field
    4. Click create

  • How do I know what to roleplay:

    • The roleplay on Elemelons is very open. All you have to do is create a character, and join the server! If you don’t know what to do with your character, you can always ask HadesTheImmortal, since he writes most of the scripts, and designed some characters you see on the server. For inspiration, you can find past roleplay events and story arcs on the site.

  • A download link is broken/unavailable:

    1. Try a other link or wait for the link to become available again.
    2. Contact us with one of the methods in the contacts tab.
